# Bugs Found During Testing

## Bug 1 – Repeated Reels
  The same Reels appear repeatedly during browsing. Even after closing and reopening the Reels feature, videos that were already watched continue to appear again after several new videos.


## Bug 2 – Freeze After Viewing a Photo Post
  After opening a photo post, the Reels feature freezes. It becomes impossible to return to the previous Reel or move to the next one.


## Bug 3 – Previously Viewed Reel Freezes
  When returning to a previously watched Reel, the video no longer plays and is displayed as a static image instead of continuing playback.

## Conclusion
  This test case focuses on the Reels feature during Instagram calls. Before testing the Reels feature, the main call features were verified to ensure they were working correctly. During testing, several issues with the Reels feature were identified.
